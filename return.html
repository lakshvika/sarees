Return & Refund Policy

Commitment to Quality & Customer Satisfaction

At Lakshvika, every saree is a labor of love, handwoven by skilled artisans. We perform a rigorous 3-step quality check before shipping any product. However, we understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations. Below is our policy regarding returns and exchanges.

1. Domestic Orders (India)

For orders delivered within India, we offer a customer-friendly return window.

7-Day Return Window

You may raise a return request within 7 days from the date of delivery.

Conditions for Return:

  • The product must be unused, unworn, and unwashed.
  • All original tags, packaging, and the authenticity certificate must be intact.
  • The saree must not have the blouse piece cut or fall/pico done.

Non-Returnable Items:

We cannot accept returns for:

  • Customized items (e.g., pre-stitched blouses, altered lengths).
  • Sarees where the blouse piece has been detached.
  • Products bought during a "Clearance Sale."

2. International Orders (Outside India)

Due to the complex nature of international shipping, customs, and import duties, our policy differs for global clients.

Final Sale Policy

All international orders are considered Final Sale and are generally not eligible for returns or exchanges due to high logistic costs and cross-border regulations.

Exceptions (Damaged Goods):

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with clear photographs. We will review the case and offer either:

  • A partial refund for minor fixable issues.
  • A full refund or replacement if the product is significantly damaged or incorrect.

Note: Lakshvika is not responsible for minor color variations due to screen resolution or natural variations in handloom weaving.

3. How to Initiate a Return (India Only)

To schedule a return pick-up, follow these steps:

  • Email us at returns@lakshvika.com or WhatsApp us at +91 9852 9852 99 within 7 days of delivery.
  • Mention your Order ID and the reason for return.
  • Our team will arrange a reverse pick-up within 2-3 business days.

4. Refunds Process

Once we receive the returned item at our Kolkata studio, it will undergo a quality check.

  • Approval: If the product passes the quality check, the refund will be initiated within 48 hours.
  • Credit Method: The amount will be refunded to your original payment source (Bank/UPI/Card).
  • Timeline: Please allow 5-7 business days for the amount to reflect in your bank account.

Need Help?

If you have any doubts regarding sizing, fabric, or color before placing an order, our designers are happy to assist you.
