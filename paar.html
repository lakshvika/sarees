The Paar Collection

(Woven Heritage): Focus on regional weaving styles from Bengal and Eastern India.

Tangail

(BENGAL WEAVE)

Fine cotton weave with heritage borders from Bengal.

Dhaniakhali

(OFF-WHITE & RED)

Renowned for its tight weave and contrasting bold borders.

Begampuri

(LOOSE WEAVE)

Distinctive loose weave with bright and broad borders.

Sambalpuri

(ODISHA IKAT)

Traditional tie-dyed ikat art reflecting eastern heritage.

Bomkai

(THREAD WORK)

Intricate extra weft technique from Ganjam district.

Shantipuri

(FINE TEXTURE)

Super fine texture with traditional jacquard borders.
